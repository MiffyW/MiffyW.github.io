Bushfire - Carsales Hackathon - Oct 2018

By : Carsales Bushfire Team   Category : Machine Learning

During my participation in the Oct. 2018 Carsales Hackathon, I worked in a team to build an experimental system to detect bushfire via image and video resources. We used Python and TensorFlow were to build the object-detection ML model. Our project won the Oct 2018 Carsales Hackathon First Prize and also a Green Award.
